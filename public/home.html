About Our App

Welcome to the Debt Collection System, your ultimate tool for managing debts and collections. Our platform provides a secure and user-friendly interface to record debts, track payments, and receive money from debtors. With features like real-time notifications and easy-to-use forms, you can stay on top of your financial obligations.

Key features include:

  • Record new debts with debtor details and due dates
  • Track payments and update debt status
  • Receive money from debtors seamlessly
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Responsive design for all devices
